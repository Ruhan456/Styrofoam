Our goal


Since these two products are fairly new to the tableware industry many retail stores may not

know about the benefits or may be reluctant to switch since it does cost slightly more, amounting to around 1 cent per cup compared to styrofoam. With the right marketing and promotion, we hope to be able to switch most if not all small and new retail businesses to sell bagasse and molded fibers instead of the regular styrofoam tableware within the first year. Our big picture plan is to attempt to switch most, optimistically all major retail businesses in Canada to using bagasse and molded fiber tableware further down the timeline. The budget would be spent on promotions such as fliers, online advertisements, improving the website, investing in more production of bagasse and molded fiber, and petitions to convince more to switch.
